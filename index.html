My secondary focus has been on artificial intelligence (AI), where I have garnered substantial experience through various undergraduate research internships. One of my notable projects involved developing a transformer AI model to detect and predict cybersickness through a virtual reality headset, achieving an accuracy rate of approximately 85%.
